About this site

Why make a site to help people find their Polling Stations?

Registered voters will be sent a polling card by their local council that will have a map on it.

But we think that in the digital age, people might also expect that information to be online, particularly if they lose the card or forget to carry it with them on the day.

We think they’re quite likely to Google ‘where’s my polling station?’, and we’d like to help them out.

Who's behind this site?

This site is part of the non-profit Democracy Club - who sourced the data from each individual Council via FOI requests.

You don't seem to have my Polling Station?

Because this site is built by volunteers who have sourced the data by requesting it from each individual Council, unfortunately we don't have 100% coverage right now.

We're very much working towards that though, and are actively working with councils up & down the country. Do feel free to drop us a line if you'd like to discuss.

I think you're sending me to the wrong polling station!

If you think we're mis-directing you, then we really want to know ! Please email us at pollingstations@democracyclub.org.uk

How can I help?

If you'd like to help us importing the data we've collected, or by helping with the front-end web development or design, just email us - there's plenty to do!

Alternatively, you can head over to the Github repository where we track issues and tasks.

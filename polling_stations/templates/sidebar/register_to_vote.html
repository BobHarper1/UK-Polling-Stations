You need to be registered in order to vote

If you aren't registered to vote visit https://www.gov.uk/register-to-vote

  • Register before midnight on Monday 22 May to vote in the UK General Election on 8 June.
  • You don't need to register again if you've already registered at your current address.
  • It is too late to register for the 4 May local elections.
